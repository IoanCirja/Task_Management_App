      Welcome to the Task Management App!

      With this app, you can manage your tasks and stay organized.

      To add a new task, click on the "Add Task" button and fill in the details.
      Your tasks will be listed as "Uncompleted tasks" by default. Once a task is completed, click on it to move it to the "Completed tasks" section.

      To edit a task, click on it and make changes in the dialog box that appears. To delete a task, click the "Delete" button.

      For any assistance, click on the "Contact" button.
